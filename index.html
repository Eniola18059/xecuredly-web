FAQ

Common Questions

What industries do you serve?

+

We serve clients across Financial Services, Healthcare, Government, Energy, Retail, Technology, and Manufacturing. Our consultants bring domain-specific knowledge so our solutions fit your regulatory and operational context — not just generic best practices.

How quickly can you start on a project?

+

For most engagements, we can have a dedicated consultant onboarded and working within 5–7 business days of signing. For emergency incident response or urgent security needs, we have consultants on standby who can mobilize within 24 hours.

Do you offer remote or on-site consulting?

+

Both. The majority of our work is delivered remotely using secure, encrypted collaboration tools. For sensitive engagements — such as physical security audits, on-premise deployments, or executive-level workshops — we provide on-site consultants globally.

How does pricing work?

+

We offer flexible engagement models: fixed-scope project pricing, monthly retainers for ongoing support, and time-and-materials for dynamic engagements. After your initial consultation, we provide a detailed proposal tailored to your scope and budget.

Are your consultants certified?

+

Yes — every Xecuredly consultant holds relevant industry certifications including CISSP, CISM, CEH, PMP, AWS/Azure, and more. We maintain strict vetting standards and only place consultants who are proven in their domain with real-world delivery track records.

What is X-Academy?

+

X-Academy is our training platform offering structured programs in cybersecurity, data analytics, AI, and project management. Programs range from 2-day intensives to 12-week bootcamps, and are available for individuals and corporate teams. All courses are taught by active practitioners.
